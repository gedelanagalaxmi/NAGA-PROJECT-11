Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add ginger garlic paste.
  2. Add chicken cubes and cook until white.
  3. Pour in tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cream, garam masala, and salt. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
